Careers at Jeh Tea Shop: Driver


Position: Driver

At Jeh Tea Shop, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional products to our customers, and our drivers play a crucial role in ensuring our premium teas, organic coffees, and gourmet treats reach their destination safely and on time. We are seeking a reliable and professional driver to join our team and help us maintain our high standards of customer service and delivery efficiency.


Key Responsibilities:

  • Safely and efficiently transport products, including teas, coffees, and baked goods, to customers, retail partners, and events.
  • Ensure all deliveries are completed in a timely manner and within scheduled delivery windows.
  • Handle all products with care, ensuring they arrive in excellent condition
  • Communicate professionally with customers, providing a friendly and helpful delivery experience.
  • Maintain accurate delivery logs and reports, tracking mileage, delivery times, and completed orders.
  • Assist with loading and unloading products at the warehouse and delivery destinations
  • Ensure that the delivery vehicle is well-maintained, clean, and in proper working condition.
  • Follow all traffic and safety regulations to ensure safe delivery.
  • Assist with warehouse operations, including organizing and packing products for delivery

Qualifications:

  • Proven experience as a delivery driver or in a similar role.
  • Valid driver’s license with a clean driving record.
  • Familiarity with the local area and ability to follow GPS navigation and delivery routes efficiently.
  • Strong customer service skills and ability to communicate professionally and courteously.
  • Ability to handle physical tasks, including lifting and moving packages.
  • Dependable and punctual, with the ability to manage time effectively.
  • Basic understanding of vehicle maintenance and care.
  • Ability to work flexible hours, including weekends and holidays if needed.

Benefits

  • Competitive hourly wage or salary based on experience.
  • Mileage reimbursement or company vehicle provided for deliveries.
  • Staff discounts on all tea, coffee, and baked products.
  • Opportunities for career growth within Jeh Tea Shop.
  • Supportive work environment with a focus on teamwork and customer satisfaction.
  • Flexible scheduling with potential for both part-time and full-time hours.
  • Paid time off and other employee benefits.

How to Apply

If you’re a dependable and customer-focused driver looking to join a growing team, we’d love to hear from you! Please submit your resume and a brief cover letter outlining your experience and why you’d be a great fit for Jeh Tea Shop’s Driver position to careers@jehteashop.com
